BTech (Bachelor of Technology)

Engineering fields like Computer Science (CS), Electronics & Communication (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), Civil Engineering, and more. Focus on technical skills, coding, and practical applications.

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

Business Administration. Covers management, finance, marketing, operations, and organizational leadership. Great for aspiring entrepreneurs and business managers.

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

Computer Applications. Focus on software development, programming languages, database management, and application design. Similar to BTech but more application-focused.

BSc (Bachelor of Science)

Science disciplines like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Environmental Science. Strong theoretical foundation for research and advanced studies.

BArch (Bachelor of Architecture)

Architecture. Design and planning of buildings and spaces. Combines creativity with technical skills and engineering principles.

MBA (Master of Business Administration)

Management. Covers business strategy, leadership, finance, marketing. Postgraduate degree for career advancement in management roles.

M.Tech (Master of Technology)

Advanced Engineering fields. Postgraduate specialization in areas like Data Science, AI/ML, Cloud Computing, and other emerging technologies.
